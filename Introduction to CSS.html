My Favourite Musical Instrument

I like to play the violin

The violin is a stringed instument

In its essence, the violin is a sounding box for strings put in vibration by a bow. What makes the violin so singularly expressive is the variety of touch and tonal inflection possible with the bow, put in motion by the right arm. The bow is truly the “soul” of the violin, its primary means of expression.

The word “violin” comes from Italian violino, a diminutive form of viola, which owes its roots to Medieval Latin vitula (“stringed instrument”).
